Chain Of Command

Commissioner - N. Nathan

The Commissioner is the most important face in SAST. Their job is to make major decisions for the department, handle reports on fellow officers, overseeing all of the department and more. They must make sure the department is well handled and under control at all times. The Assistant Commissioner is able to give a helping hand to the Commissioner to help them when needed.

Assistant Commissioner - M. Homicidal

The Assistant Commissioner's job in SAST is to help the Commissioner with anything they need help with like new rules, handle reports on fellow officers, new 1K’s, changing SAST information, etc. The Assistant Commissioner is also in charge of maintaining the people joining SAST and making sure we promote it well and have growth.

Deputy Commissioner - N/A

The Deputy Commissioner is responsible for helping and assisting with anything that the higher-ups need. They are also assigned to the Colonel's job as well as helping the department heads with decisions such as implementing new things in SAST, promoting people to Lieutenant Colonel, Commander, etc.

Colonel II - M. Mel

The Colonel's job inside SAST is to manage all of SASTs questions that our Lieutenant Colonel's has passed up to Colonel along with assisting the high command with anything they need such as promotions and Pre-Command’s job.

Colonel I - N/A

The Colonel's job inside SAST is to manage all of SAST's questions that our Lieutenant Colonel's has passed up to Colonel along with assisting the high command with anything they need such as promotions and Pre-Command’s job.

Lieutenant Colonels

The Lieutenant Colonel's job inside SAST is to oversee our Commanders making sure they are on task and also assisting with Quote of The Day, Photo Of The Week, Promotions and more.
